Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

MERI DIET is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, and information that is automatically collected when you use our services.

  • Account Information: We collect your username, email address, and phone number when you register.
  • Health Information: We collect health-related data you provide to our chatbot, such as age, weight, height, health goals, and medical conditions, to generate personalized diet plans.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Generate personalized diet plans.
  • Communicate with you, including responding to your comments and questions.

3. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we have on you. You can update your account information through your profile settings.

Note: This is a template privacy policy. For a commercial website, it is highly recommended to consult with a legal professional.
